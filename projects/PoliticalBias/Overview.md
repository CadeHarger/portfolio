
I used the same prediction system that I'd built for the Kalshi bot to test the influence of politically biased news articles upon the LLM-predicted outcome for related political events. I was unable to find significant effects of this leading to bias in predictions. This might seem like there's no finding, but the absense of evidence suggests LLMs could be good at filtering bias from such signal, unlike humans. 

I began writing a paper for this, but ultimately decided not to further pursue this for the following reasons:
- The findings weren't great: I would've had to gone through more experimentation and find results that I may not get in order to prove an association suggesting that LLMs could filter bias instead of just the absense of evidence that there is bias.
- The publishing process is long and I'm not in school anymore
- My heart wasn't in it; I was using AI to write a lot of the paper and had to constantly go back and write it in my own words. I figured if I feel the need to use AI to blaze through it, I should probably not be doing it.
